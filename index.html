Your four phases

Every phase is a performance advantage.
If you know how to use it.

Aligna maps your menstrual cycle in real time and tells you — in plain language — exactly what your body and mind are optimized for today.

Phase 1
Menstrual
Days 1–5
Your reset and reflection phase. Intuition is sharpest here. Give yourself permission to think, not push — this is where clarity comes from.
Phase 2
Follicular
Days 6–13
Use follicular energy to start what actually matters. Your brain is primed for new ideas, bold plans, and creative output. This is your execution launchpad.
Phase 3
Ovulatory
Days 14–17
Your peak performance window. Schedule your biggest pitch during ovulation — and land it. Energy, confidence, and communication are all at their highest.
Phase 4
Luteal
Days 18–28
Stop forcing decisions during luteal phase — and avoid costly mistakes. Your detail orientation is high, but major decisions belong in a different window.

This is how high-performing women stop working harder — and start working smarter.
